How to Play

  • Drag the tiles to the game board.

  • Tiles must be placed directly next to one another.

  • After making a word, press the next word button to submit it.

  • To remove a tile from the board, click the tile to bring it back to the rack.

  • Words must be at least 2 letters long.

  • To get new tiles, press the new tiles button.
